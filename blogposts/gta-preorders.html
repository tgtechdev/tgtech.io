GTA 6 Pre-orders

So the other day, I pre-ordered Grand Theft Auto 6. I've heard mixed opinions about pre-ordering on websites such as Reddit and Tiktok - some positive and some not. Of course, with the absoloute train wreck that was Cyberpunk 2077 when it came out, lots of people are cautious for a good reason about pre-orders.

I'm not expecting perfection from Rockstar. But I am expecting the bare minimum of the game running (not perfectly but it has to at least run somewhat somoothly), a decent story and plotline and no exessive microtransactons. This is one of, if not the biggest and most anticipated game of all time and a lot of pressure and expectations have been put on the developers.

But with a wait time of almost 13 years and a budget of 2 billion dollars, they have had plenty of time to work with concepts, story, map desing and gameplay that lots of people won't take any excuses for a bad game.
