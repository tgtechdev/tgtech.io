My Top Five Games

here are some of my favourite games i like to play. what do you think?

1: Kingdom Come Deliverance 2

This is my favourite game because of the story, gameplay, weapons and armour. If you want a in depth review, check put my blog post on it. It has been over a year since I have started to play this game and I have obtained over 400 hours of gameplay over multiple playthroughs.

Check on PlayStation Store

2: Cyberpunk 2077

I like Cyberpunk because of the story, lore, characters, awesome cybernetic implants you can get and the futuristic location of Night City.

Now I know this game had a bad launch but after a few years, it is now much better and a really good game. It will still have a bug or two there but nothing astronomical.

Check on PlayStation Store

3: Kingdom Come Deliverance 1

The prequel to KCD2, I like this game because of the DLCs, atmosphere, and every other thing I said about KCD2. But there are some things I like about this game then the sequel: I think some aspects of combat are better in my opinion; the overall atmosphere of the game just feels more real or just more medieval, but I do think a lot of other things are still a bit clunky.

Check on PlayStation Store

4: Red Dead Redemption 2

This will always be in my top five (or ten) in my opinion. Simply because its the first ever proper game I've played but there are games I just enjoy better. Even after almost 10 years since its release date, this game still holds up - the graphics are phenomenal, gameplay is awesome and the story is just great.

Check on PlayStation Store

5: God of War Ragnarök

I platinum'd this game back in 2023. I enjoyed (almost) every part of it apart from the berserkers and the Valkyrie but apart from those boss battles. I really enjoyed this game and the one before it, too. I just love how Kratos grows as a person and as a father, including the dynamic between Atreus and Kratos and how both of them have changed since the last game. I cannot wait for the old trilogy to be remade.

Check on PlayStation Store
